About the Game

Call of Duty: Mobile (CODM) delivers a high-energy FPS experience optimized for mobile devices. Players can jump into classic game modes like Team Deathmatch, Search and Destroy, Domination, and Battle Royale, all set in legendary maps from across the franchise. With seasonal updates, operator skills, weapon upgrades, and unique character skins, CODM lets you customize your playstyle and compete in action-packed matches. Whether you're grinding ranked mode or enjoying casual battles with friends, CODM offers nonstop excitement and strategic gameplay on the go.
